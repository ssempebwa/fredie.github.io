KABIRA COUNTRY CLUB

Kabira Country Club is a the perfect venue for various events like business meetings, weddings or a relaxing getaway.

Business hotels in Kampala: Kabira Country Club is a business and luxury hotel situated in Kampala's Northern suburb of Bukoto, 5 km drive from City Centre. The 4 star hotel boasts an outdoor swimming pool, a well-equipped gym with sauna and steam room, a massage center, tennis and squash courts. High speed free Wi Fi is available. 

Kabira Country Club has 95 unique rooms, suites and cottages; each with a separate shower and bathtub, living room, dining area, private balcony and an electronic private safe. The rooms are designed with glazed hardwood flooring.
